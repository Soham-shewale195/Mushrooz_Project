CrispyZ

Oyster mushrooms (Pleurotus ostreatus) are one of the most popular edible mushrooms known for their oyster-shaped caps and delicate flavor. They are soft, tender, and slightly chewy in texture, making them a great addition to various dishes like soups, stir-fries, and pastas. Grown naturally on decaying wood or cultivated on straw and sawdust, oyster mushrooms are easy to grow and highly sustainable. They are rich in protein, fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, offering several health benefits such as boosting immunity, lowering cholesterol, and supporting brain health. Due to their low calorie and glycemic index, they are also suitable for diabetic and weight-conscious individuals. Their mild, earthy taste and impressive nutrition make oyster mushrooms a favorite in both culinary and medicinal use across the world.
