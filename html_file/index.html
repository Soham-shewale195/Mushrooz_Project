Benefits of Oyster Mushroom Powder:

Oyster mushroom powder is a nutrient-rich supplement that offers several health benefits. Some of the potential benefits include:

1. Immune System Support: Oyster mushrooms contain beta-glucans, which can stimulate the immune system and increase its ability to fight off infections.
2. Antioxidant Properties: Oyster mushrooms are rich in antioxidants, which can help protect cells from damage caused by free radicals.
3. Anti-Inflammatory Effects: Oyster mushrooms have been shown to have anti-inflammatory properties, which may help reduce inflammation and improve overall health.
4. Cardiovascular Health: The potassium content in oyster mushrooms can help lower blood pressure and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.
5. Digestive Health: Oyster mushrooms are a good source of dietary fibre, which can help promote digestive health and prevent constipation.
