About Us

The MushrooZ

A Warm Greetings from Mushroo'Z. We specialize in the cultivation and production of premium-quality oyster mushrooms.

With a strong focus on sustainable and innovative farming techniques, we provide a diverse range of oyster mushroom varieties including: Pleurotus Djamor, Pleurotus Ostreatus, Pleurotus Citrinopileatus, and Pleurotus Sajor Caju.

Our mushrooms are grown in controlled, eco-friendly environments to ensure top-tier quality and freshness. Whether you're a restaurant, grocery store, or individual looking for healthy and nutritious gourmet mushrooms, Mushroo'Z is your trusted partner for all oyster mushroom needs.

We are committed to delivering the best in taste, texture, and consistency, while promoting environmental sustainability and supporting local farmers.
