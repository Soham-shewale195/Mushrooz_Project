About Mushrooms

Mushrooms are fungi that come in many shapes, sizes, and colors. Some are edible and packed with nutrients, others are toxic.

Popular Types of Mushrooms

  • Button Mushroom: Mild, common.
  • Shiitake: Meaty, used in Asian dishes.
  • Oyster Mushroom: Delicate, stir-fry friendly.
