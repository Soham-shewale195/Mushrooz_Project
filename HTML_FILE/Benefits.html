🍄 Health Benefits of Mushrooms

Mushrooms are called functional foods because they offer both nutrition and medicinal value.

  • • Low in Calories: Ideal for weight management, <50 cal per 100g.
  • • Rich in Antioxidants: Contains selenium, ergothioneine, glutathione.
  • • Vitamins B & D: Energy, brain health, and bone support.
  • • Anti-inflammatory Effects: Contains beta-glucans, triterpenoids.
  • • High in Fiber: Improves digestion, controls sugar & cholesterol.
  • • Plant-Based Protein: 1.5–3g per 100g — good for vegetarians.
  • • Essential Nutrients: Potassium, selenium, copper, zinc.
  • • Low Fat & Sugar: Diabetic-friendly, heart-healthy.
  • 🍄 Reishi: Immunity boost, stress relief, liver support.
  • 🍄 Lion’s Mane: Brain function, nerve repair, focus.
  • 🍄 Chaga: Antioxidant-rich, immune support.
  • 🍄 Cordyceps: Increases energy, lung health.
  • 🍄 Turkey Tail: Cancer support, gut health.
