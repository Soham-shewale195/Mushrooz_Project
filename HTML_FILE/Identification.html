Identification Guide

How to Identify Edible vs. Poisonous Mushrooms

Look for known characteristics like gills, cap shape, and habitat. Avoid mushrooms with white gills, skirt, or bulbous base unless identified with certainty.

Safety Tips

  • Never eat wild mushrooms without expert confirmation.
  • Use a mushroom field guide or mobile app.
  • When in doubt, throw it out!
